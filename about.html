MAKING AND DISTRIBUTING LEAN TOOLS AND EQUIPMENT

We have lean/kaizen tools which make work more comfortable with little cost.
For example, Katakuri kaizen leverages on using kinetic energy to move objects. Lean tools share some universal principles (below) with katakuri tools.
We make and supply such tools/equipment after assessing your house/office/business needs. We also do assist in fitting the tools/equipment in place. 

  • Don’t use the human hand. Move objects automatically.
  • Don’t spend money.
  • Use the force of your equipment.
  • Build it with the wisdom and creativity of the people on the shop floor.
  • For safety, don’t just rely on paying attention but build a device that stops automatically.
